Mother Earth's Plant Boutique

Mother Earth's Plant Boutique's mission is to promote the joys of horticulture and help people to become successful plant owners. The boutique sells a variety of handselected, healthy houseplants and everything you need to take care of them, and also offers workshops and private services.
